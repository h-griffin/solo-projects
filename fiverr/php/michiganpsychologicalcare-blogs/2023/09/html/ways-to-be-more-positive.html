Ways to Be More Positive

Not all of us are programmed to see our glass as half-full naturally. However, those who can see the world through a more optimistic lens have been found to experience more benefits to their mental health, overall wellbeing, and satisfaction with life.

Does that mean all hope is lost if you are not a natural optimist? Of course not! In today’s article, we will cover ways that you can begin to see the world with more positivity and optimism than ever before!

The Benefits of Positivity

Positivity allows you to view the world from a better stance, which can increase your overall life satisfaction and offer some great mental health benefits. These benefits include:

  • Improved self-esteem and confidence
  • Lower risk of mortality
  • More resiliency in hard or stressful times
  • Greater cardiovascular health
  • A stronger immune system
  • Lower risk of depression

How to Be More Optimistic

Being optimistic doesn’t come easy for everyone, but that doesn’t mean you are not capable of viewing the world from a more positive perspective. A few ways to be more optimistic include:

Learn to Catch Your Negative Thoughts

Becoming more conscious of your negative thoughts will be the first step to change them into neutral or positive ones.

Stop Blaming Yourself

Approach shortcomings with compassion and patience. Treat yourself the way you would a friend. Viewing yourself in a more positive light is the first step to viewing the world more optimistically.

Practice Mindfulness and Intentionality

When you are mindful and intentional with your thoughts, you can have greater control over them. Journaling and meditation are some of the most popular forms of mindfulness, but there are many different methods you can use.

Work to Make Negative Thoughts More Neutral or Positive

Part of being more optimistic involves making a conscious effort to change your thoughts. This can certainly take some time and practice, but catching negative thoughts and learning to change them to more neutral or positive ones will be a huge step toward approaching life more optimistically.

Try to Face Life with More Humor

Sometimes, laughter really is the greatest medicine. When you make a mistake, face a failure, or are having a hard day, choosing to view the situation through the lens of humor can be a great way to prevent you from spiraling down a negative rabbit hole.

Take Care of Your Health

When your physical health is thriving, your mental health will thrive, too. A few ways to take care of your physical health include:

  • Sleeping enough
  • Eating well
  • Exercising daily
  • Drinking enough water

Surround Yourself with Optimists

The people you surround yourself with have a huge role in the way you approach the world. Surrounding yourself with positive people will help you become more positive, too!

Practice Makes Perfect

The more you practice thinking positively—whether through changing your thoughts, positive meditation, journaling, or practicing gratitude—the better chance you will have at developing a more positive affect.
