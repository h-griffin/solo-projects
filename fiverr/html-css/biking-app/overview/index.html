What is the Derailleur app?

Derailleur is the quickest and easiest way to estimate your cycling speed at different cadences depending on the setup of your bicycle. Designed to be the best and easiest-to-use app for calculating gear ratios and skid patches, Derailleur combines precise calculations with an intuitive design, so that changing your bicycle setup is quick and simple.
