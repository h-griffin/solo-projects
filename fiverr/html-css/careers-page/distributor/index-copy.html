A high-growth leader

Colabor is a leading distributor of food and related products. The Company distributes 35,000 products to 15,000 institutions and businesses in Quebec and Atlantic.

Colabor was founded in 1962 as a buying group. In 2005, it became a publicly traded company. In recent years, Colabor has made a series of acquisitions. Its expansion strategy, driven by its ambitious and rigorous vision, has borne fruit: the Company enjoys sustained growth and continues to strengthen its market position.
